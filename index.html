Consumer

Key Tailwinds

Technology Transformation

Pervasive cloud migration, enterprise AI adoption accelerating digital transformation investments, increasing cybersecurity spending, and specialization creating opportunities for vertical solutions addressing unique industry needs and compliance requirements.

Healthcare

Healthcare technology and provider services

Key Tailwinds

Accelerating digital health adoption, mounting clinical and operational pressures on providers, regulatory drivers for interoperability and data exchange, artificial intelligence applications in diagnostics and treatment optimization, and market consolidation creating investment opportunities.

Differentiated Offerings

We partner with healthcare organizations demonstrating strong clinical outcomes, established provider relationships, sustainable competitive advantages, and management teams focused on delivering value across quality, access, and cost dimensions.

Technology Transformation

Clinical intelligence platforms leveraging AI for diagnostics and treatment optimization, agentic AI automating administrative workflows and prior authorization, workflow automation improving care coordination, process intelligence enabling operational optimization, and interoperability solutions improving data exchange across care settings.

Differentiated Offerings

We invest in industrial companies with specialized engineering expertise, established customer relationships, operational excellence, proven track records navigating commodity cycles, and capabilities to drive continuous improvement and cost optimization.

Technology Transformation

Process intelligence enabling predictive maintenance and throughput optimization, workflow automation reducing manual labor requirements, agentic AI optimizing supply chain planning, AI-powered analytics improving production efficiency, and digital tools enhancing real-time visibility and decision-making across operations.
